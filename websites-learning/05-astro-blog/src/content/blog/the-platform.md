date: 2024-12-12

The web is the most forgiving runtime in the world. You can open DevTools, type something, and see it instantly. That's why these small sites make such good learning projects.

Three things I'd focus on first:

1. **Semantic HTML** — it pays off forever.
2. **Modern CSS** — Grid, Flexbox, and custom properties cover 95% of layouts.
3. **One framework, deeply** — don't learn five frameworks shallowly.

The other sites in this repo (vanilla, React, Vue, Svelte) are short on purpose. Open one, read it, change it, break it.
